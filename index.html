About this project

    
    
Virginia Racialization Condition map contains information about racial distribution, income level and degree of education. It helps with analyzing social and economic condition of virginia. This map is an assignment created in the Scripting Civic Engagement seminar at the University of Virginia (Fall 2018), taught by Andrea Hansen Phillips. The map was made by Qiyao Li.
Map data courtesy of Mapbox Studio and the United States Census Bereau
For questions or to improve this map, contact Qiyao Li
